# Encryption & Signing

## Encryption (confidentiality)

- Asymmetric scheme
  - public key encrypts
  - private key decrypts
- Symmetric scheme
  - secret key encrypts and decrypts

## Signing/HMAC (integrity & authenticity)

- Asymmetric scheme
  - private key signs
  - public key verifies
- Symmetric scheme
  - secret key signs and verifies

## Both

- Combined scheme
